Frequently Asked Questions

What types of AI/ML projects do you offer, and can they be customized to meet my college requirements?

We offer AI and ML projects across 14 sectors, including healthcare, retail and commerce, text and image processing, and more. With over 50 projects already implemented using Machine Learning and Deep Learning, we are well-equipped to deliver high-quality solutions. We are ready to work on any project based on your needs and ideas, strictly adhering to your college requirements. Unlike pre-made templates, every project is fully customized to meet your specific demands.

Will I receive complete project documentation, including code comments, reports, and presentations?

This is where many people trust us, making us a leader in the market. We provide complete project documentation, strictly following your college's template. Additionally, we offer a ready-made PowerPoint presentation to help you confidently deliver your final presentation. Every step of the program is thoroughly commented for better understanding of the code. We also provide an option to write a research paper for your project at an additional cost.

How long will it take to deliver the project, and do you offer urgent delivery options?

For pre-implemented projects, we deliver within a week, including implementation, deployment (as a website or app), customized PPT, and documentation. For customized projects built on your ideas, delivery takes up to 14 days. We also accommodate urgent delivery requests at no additional cost.

Do you provide guidance on how to explain and present the project during my viva or evaluation?

This is where our expertise truly shines, ensuring complete client satisfaction. We offer on-call support to address any doubts and provide detailed explanations of every line of the code during long calls. Additionally, we recommend potential viva questions along with detailed answers to help you prepare. We will be with you every step of the way until you successfully complete your project.

What is the cost of a project, and are there any hidden charges?

We charge significantly less compared to others in the market, offering complex and advanced projects at an affordable price—often costing less than a smartwatch. There are no hidden charges, and you get high-quality work at a budget-friendly price. Please contact us to know more about our pricing details.

Do you ensure the project is original and adheres to academic integrity standards?

Yes, we ensure complete originality in every project. We use different techniques and datasets to make each project unique. By incorporating your ideas, we align the project with your vision while maintaining its originality. Every step, from dataset collection to model deployment, is implemented by us without copying any existing work, ensuring adherence to academic integrity standards.

What happens if I encounter issues with the project after delivery? Do you provide technical support?

We promise to support you until you successfully complete your project. No matter how many queries you have, we are here to address them every time. We provide comprehensive technical support and make necessary changes, even after delivering the project, to ensure a smooth experience.

Can I request modifications after the project is delivered, and will there be extra charges?

You can request any major modifications after the project delivery, and we won’t charge for them unless you request to completely change the project itself. (Just kidding!) We’re here to ensure the project meets your expectations without unnecessary extra costs.

What payment methods do you accept, and do you offer installment options?

We accept all UPI payments, and you can pay in two installments: one at the time of the agreement and the second after the project is delivered. This flexible payment option makes it easy and convenient for you to manage the cost.

How will this project benefit my career or academic journey in AI/ML?

Our projects go beyond typical final-year projects—they follow all the necessary steps that an ML or Data Science project involves. These are real-time, industry-oriented, and research-based projects. We help you understand how academic concepts are implemented in real-world scenarios, giving you valuable insights into their practical applications. This hands-on experience will not only enhance your academic skills but also prepare you for a successful career in AI/ML.
